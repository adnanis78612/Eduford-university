Courses We Offer

These are the main classes we offer at our university.

COMPUTER SCIENCE :

This course explores the fundamentals of computing, including programming, algorithms, and software development. Students will learn how to analyze problems, design solutions, and implement them using various programming languages. Emphasizing both theory and practical skills, the course prepares you for careers in technology and innovation, equipping you with the tools needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

ECONOMICS :

This course in economics covers fundamental principles of economic theory, including supply and demand, market structures, and economic policies. Students will analyze real-world issues like inflation and unemployment while developing critical thinking and analytical skills. By understanding how economies function, this course prepares students for careers in business, finance, and public policy.

PSYCHOLOGY :

This course in psychology explores the fundamental concepts of human behavior and mental processes. Students will examine key theories related to cognition, emotion, and social interactions, as well as topics like development, personality, and mental health. Through a combination of lectures and practical applications, the course aims to enhance understanding of psychological principles and prepares students for careers in counseling, research, and various fields that require insights into human behavior.
